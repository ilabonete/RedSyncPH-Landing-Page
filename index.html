Built on Evidence, Designed for Impact

RedSync PH addresses documented challenges in Philippine hemophilia care through evidence-based design

Geographic Barriers

Long travel distances to treatment centers in Mindanao, particularly affecting rural patients seeking specialized care

Fragmented Scheduling

Absence of reliable, real-time clinic availability information leading to missed consultations and delayed care

Manual Record-Keeping

Reliance on handwritten notebooks for tracking, prone to errors and limiting healthcare provider decision-making

Limited Specialist Access

Shortage of hematologists and hemophilia specialists, compounded by healthcare system fragmentation

Important Medical Disclaimer

RedSync PH is a digital health tool designed to support hemophilia management but is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare providers for medical decisions. The app's AI features provide educational information only and should not replace clinical judgment. In case of medical emergencies, contact emergency services immediately.

Research Partnership: Developed in collaboration with the Hemophilia Association in Davao (HAnD) through participatory design methodology. Information verified against WHO and World Federation of Hemophilia guidelines.
